$X^4 + X^3 -2X +1$ & $X^2 + X +1$ & $-X-2$   & $7$ \\ \hline
                   & $X^2 - 1$    & $-X-3$   & \\ \hline
$1$                & $0$          & $1$      & $U_3$\\ \hline
$0$                & $1$          & $-X^2+1$ & $V_3$ \\ \hline

 U_3 = X + 3, \; V_3 = -X^3 -3 X^2 +X +4.
\]

\[
 \left\lbrace \left( \frac{1}{7}U_0+\Lambda B, \frac{1}{7}V_0-\Lambda A\right), \Lambda \in \R[X] \right\rbrace
