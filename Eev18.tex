\begin{tiny}(Eev18)\end{tiny} Soit $A$, $B$, $C$, $D$ des sous-espaces vectoriels. Montrer que:
\begin{displaymath}
 \left. 
\begin{aligned}
 A+B &= C+D \\ A &\subset C \\ B &\subset D \\ C\cap D &= \{0_E\}
\end{aligned}
\right\rbrace \Rightarrow
\left\lbrace 
\begin{aligned}
 A=C \\ B=D
\end{aligned}
\right. 
\end{displaymath}
