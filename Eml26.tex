\begin{tiny}(Eml26)\end{tiny} Soit $E$, $F$, $G$ des $\K$-espaces vectoriels et $f\in \mathcal{L}(E,F)$, $g\in \mathcal{L}(F,G)$, $h\in \mathcal{L}(G,F)$, $k\in \mathcal{L}(F,E)$. Montrer que
\[
 \left. 
 \begin{aligned}
  f &= h \circ g \circ f \\ 
  g &= g \circ f \circ k
 \end{aligned}
\right\rbrace 
\Rightarrow \ker g \oplus \Im f = F.
\]
